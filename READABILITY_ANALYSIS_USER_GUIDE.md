# Readability & SEO Analysis User Guide

## Overview

The Readability & SEO Analysis feature provides real-time feedback on your content's SEO optimization and readability. As you write, MeowSEO analyzes your content and provides actionable suggestions to improve your search rankings and reader engagement.

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Analysis Panel

1. Open a post or page in the WordPress editor
2. Look for the **MeowSEO** panel in the right sidebar
3. You'll see two main sections:
   - **Content Score Widget**: Overall SEO and Readability scores
   - **Readability Panel**: Detailed readability analysis

### Understanding Your Scores

#### Overall Scores (0-100)

- **70-100**: Excellent - Your content is well-optimized
- **40-69**: Good - Your content needs some improvements
- **0-39**: Needs Work - Focus on the recommendations below

#### SEO Score

Measures how well your content is optimized for search engines. Factors include:
- Keyword placement (title, description, headings)
- Keyword density (frequency in content)
- Content length
- Link structure
- Schema markup

#### Readability Score

Measures how easy your content is to read. Factors include:
- Sentence length
- Paragraph length
- Use of transition words
- Passive voice usage
- Heading distribution

## SEO Analysis Details

### Keyword Optimization

**What it checks:**
- Does your focus keyword appear in the title?
- Is it in the meta description?
- Is it in the first paragraph?
- How often does it appear in the content?

**How to improve:**
- Include your keyword naturally in the title
- Add it to the meta description
- Mention it in the first 100 words
- Maintain keyword density between 0.5-2.5%

### Content Length

**What it checks:**
- Total word count of your content

**Optimal range:** 300-2500 words

**How to improve:**
- Aim for at least 300 words for basic posts
- Longer content (1000+ words) typically ranks better
- Don't artificially inflate word count

### Link Structure

**Internal Links:**
- Links to other posts on your site
- Should use descriptive anchor text
- Target: 3+ internal links per post

**External Links:**
- Links to authoritative external sites
- Should be relevant to your content
- Helps establish credibility

**How to improve:**
- Link to related posts on your site
- Link to authoritative sources
- Use descriptive link text (not "click here")

### Images & Alt Text

**What it checks:**
- Do your images have alt text?
- Does alt text include your keyword?
- Coverage percentage

**How to improve:**
- Add descriptive alt text to all images
- Include your keyword in 1-2 image alt texts
- Describe what the image shows

### Direct Answer

**What it checks:**
- Is a Direct Answer field populated?
- Is it 300-450 characters?

**Why it matters:**
- Google uses Direct Answers for AI Overviews
- Increases visibility in search results

**How to improve:**
- Write a concise answer to the main question
- Keep it between 300-450 characters
- Make it clear and informative

### Schema Markup

**What it checks:**
- Is a Schema Type configured?

**Available types:**
- Article
- FAQ Page
- How-To
- Product
- Local Business

**How to improve:**
- Select the appropriate schema type
- Fill in all required fields
- Verify with Google's Rich Results Test

## Readability Analysis Details

### Sentence Length

**What it checks:**
- Average number of words per sentence

**Optimal range:** Less than 20 words per sentence

**Why it matters:**
- Short sentences are easier to read
- Improves comprehension
- Better for mobile readers

**How to improve:**
- Break long sentences into shorter ones
- Aim for 15-20 words average
- Vary sentence length for rhythm

### Paragraph Length

**What it checks:**
- Average number of words per paragraph

**Optimal range:** Less than 150 words per paragraph

**Why it matters:**
- Short paragraphs are less intimidating
- Easier to scan
- Better for mobile viewing

**How to improve:**
- Break long paragraphs into 2-3 shorter ones
- Use white space to improve readability
- Aim for 50-150 words per paragraph

### Transition Words

**What it checks:**
- Percentage of sentences with transition words
- Examples: "However", "Therefore", "Moreover", "Additionally"

**Target:** More than 30% of sentences

**Why it matters:**
- Helps readers follow your logic
- Improves flow between ideas
- Makes content more professional

**How to improve:**
- Use transition words to connect ideas
- Examples: "However", "Therefore", "Moreover", "Additionally"
- Vary your transitions to avoid repetition

### Passive Voice

**What it checks:**
- Percentage of sentences using passive voice
- Example: "The content was written" (passive) vs "I wrote the content" (active)

**Optimal range:** Less than 10% passive voice

**Why it matters:**
- Active voice is more engaging
- Clearer who is doing the action
- More direct and powerful

**How to improve:**
- Use active voice when possible
- Change "was written by" to "wrote"
- Make your writing more direct

### Heading Distribution

**What it checks:**
- Average spacing between headings
- Number of headings in content

**Optimal range:** Heading every 200-300 words

**Why it matters:**
- Helps readers scan content
- Improves SEO structure
- Better for accessibility

**How to improve:**
- Add headings every 200-300 words
- Use H2 and H3 headings
- Make headings descriptive

### Flesch Reading Ease Score

**What it is:**
- A readability score from 0-100
- Higher scores = easier to read

**Score interpretation:**
- 90-100: Very Easy (5th grade level)
- 80-89: Easy (6th grade level)
- 70-79: Fairly Easy (7th grade level)
- 60-69: Standard (8th-9th grade level)
- 50-59: Fairly Difficult (10th-12th grade level)
- 30-49: Difficult (College level)
- 0-29: Very Difficult (College graduate level)

**Target:** 60-70 for most web content

## Indonesian Language Support

The analysis engine includes special support for Indonesian language:

- **Stemming**: Recognizes word variations (e.g., "mengoptimalkan" → "optim")
- **Passive Voice Detection**: Identifies Indonesian passive patterns (di-, ter-, ke-an)
- **Transition Words**: Includes Indonesian transition words (namun, tetapi, oleh karena itu, selain itu)
- **Syllable Counting**: Accurate for Indonesian words

## Tips for Better Scores

### Quick Wins

1. **Add your keyword to the title** (+8% SEO)
2. **Include it in the first paragraph** (+8% SEO)
3. **Break long sentences** (+20% Readability)
4. **Add more headings** (+20% Readability)
5. **Write a Direct Answer** (+6% SEO)

### Content Optimization Checklist

- [ ] Focus keyword in title
- [ ] Focus keyword in meta description
- [ ] Focus keyword in first paragraph
- [ ] Keyword density 0.5-2.5%
- [ ] Content length 300-2500 words
- [ ] 3+ internal links
- [ ] External links to authoritative sites
- [ ] Alt text on all images
- [ ] Direct Answer populated
- [ ] Schema Type selected
- [ ] Average sentence length <20 words
- [ ] Average paragraph length <150 words
- [ ] 30%+ sentences with transition words
- [ ] <10% passive voice
- [ ] Heading every 200-300 words

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Keyword stuffing**: Don't overuse your keyword (>2.5%)
2. **Too long sentences**: Keep them under 20 words
3. **Wall of text**: Break content into short paragraphs
4. **No headings**: Add headings every 200-300 words
5. **Missing alt text**: Describe all images
6. **No internal links**: Link to related content
7. **Passive voice**: Use active voice when possible
8. **No transition words**: Connect your ideas

## Real-Time Analysis

### How It Works

1. You type or edit content
2. MeowSEO waits 800ms after you stop typing
3. Analysis runs in the background (doesn't block editing)
4. Results update in real-time
5. Scores and recommendations appear instantly

### Performance

- Analysis completes in 1-2 seconds
- Works with content up to 5000+ words
- No impact on editor performance
- Runs in background (Web Worker)

## Troubleshooting

### Analysis Not Updating

**Problem:** Scores aren't changing when I edit content

**Solutions:**
1. Wait 800ms after typing (debounce delay)
2. Check browser console for errors
3. Refresh the page
4. Try a different browser

### Scores Seem Wrong

**Problem:** Scores don't match my expectations

**Solutions:**
1. Check each analyzer result for details
2. Verify keyword is spelled correctly
3. Check content length (must be >50 words)
4. Ensure HTML is valid

### Performance Issues

**Problem:** Editor is slow when analyzing

**Solutions:**
1. This shouldn't happen (analysis runs in background)
2. Check browser console for errors
3. Try disabling other plugins
4. Update to latest MeowSEO version

## Glossary

- **Keyword Density**: Percentage of words that are your focus keyword
- **Flesch Reading Ease**: Readability score (0-100)
- **Passive Voice**: Sentence structure where subject receives action
- **Transition Words**: Words that connect ideas (however, therefore, etc.)
- **Schema Markup**: Structured data that helps search engines understand content
- **Alt Text**: Description of an image for accessibility and SEO
- **Direct Answer**: Concise answer to the main question
- **Internal Link**: Link to another page on your site
- **External Link**: Link to a page on another site
